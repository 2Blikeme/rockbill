RockBill – Our Story So Far

Founded in 2022, RockBill, The Neighborhood Record Store, is celebrating its twentieth anniversary of providing the greatest music and merch online!

Your Neighborhood Record Store

Our founders grew up in the 70s and 80s and The Neighborhood Record Store was the center of the music scene and the most important destination to meet friends and socialize around music. This was a place where music lovers of any age could gather and discuss their favorite genres, bands, and the latest hits and hidden music gems for hours. We are excited to keep this tradition alive by way of our fast-evolving Online Neighborhood Record Store!

Take your Favorite Music with you. Wear it!

Our apparel department has a t-shirt, hoodie, bandana or tank top for virtually every band or artist that has made a mark on popular culture. Our apparel comes in all sizes – for babies, kids, men and women, including larger sizes.

On orders over $50 USD, we ship for FREE!

Rock is about giving, so we want to share The Neighborhood Record Store experience with the whole world. We make it easy to buy your vinyl records and epic rock merch and fashion apparel from us! With a minimum order of just $50 USD, we will ship for free throughout the United States and even internationally.
Dare to compare our customer experience and our low rates and you will find that your Friendly Neighborhood Record Store is the best place to buy vinyl and rock merchandise online!
